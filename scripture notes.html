Scripture notes because I am bored

  • 1054: Roman Catholic church and Eastern Orthodox Church split
  • Church: A group of people who belong to God
    • Comes from the Greek word “ekklesia,” meaning a local meeting
  • catholic (little c): universal
  • Catholic (big C): Roman Catholic
  • We simply can't understand everything
  • The universe is huge
  • Aliens existing doesn't change our beliefs of God
  • God has no beginning, and no end; Exists outside of time
  • Why did God create us?
    • We have power to rule over rest of creation (dominion)
    • We are created in the image of God
    • God desires to rule over our hearts but still gives us free will so we can actively choose
  • What is sin?
    • Sin is just missing the mark; actions that are not in accordance with God
    • God does not punish us, we choose to live without him
  • The promise and fulfillment of Jesus
    • Throughout the stories in the old testament, God calls to us, and we constantly reject him and break the covenents
      • Leads to the promise to David of his descendant being the future forever king (Jesus)
    • Eventually God sends this king (Jesus) to be sinless, and to take on our sin to show that death and sin do not need to be the end of the story
